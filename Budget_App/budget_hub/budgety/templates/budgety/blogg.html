Paycheck to paycheck

Pay check to paycheck living is usually a sign of not being in control of your money
However, we recognize that this isnt always the case. what is in your control is how you react to the situation. If you are currently living paycheck to paycheck, whether it is your fault or not, you have the power to get yourself out of the situation.

Pay off your unnecessary Debts

Unneccessary debts is any debt that you didnt need to take on to further your financial goals. This is different for everyone but it could be purchasing something you didnt need on a credit card or financing a vacation ou couldnt afford.
